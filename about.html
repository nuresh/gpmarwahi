ABOUT

Government Polytechnic Marwahi is established in the year 2024 in the district of Gaurela Pendra Marwahi. The institute is a 100% state government funded body imparting technical education to students of tribal area of Gaurela Pendra Marwahi. lt is situated in village Kumhari, district Gaurela Pendra Marwahi. The nearest railway station is Pendra Road which is 41 KM away from the institute and nearest airport is Bilasa Devi Kevat Airport at a distance of 145 KM from Government polytechnic Marwahi.
Vision: To emerge as a leading technical institute by imparting value based education to create industry competent professionals.
Mission: Thrive to achieve excellence through:
  • Building a quality outcome-oriented learning system with well updated infrastructure and industry collaboration benefitting the learners and the society.
  • lmparting world class technical education with continuously updated knowledge base to create industrial experts.
  • Working towards holistic development of students by promoting to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities along with regular course curriculum.
Objective: Prioritize the promotion of skill based technical education in the backward area of Marwahi in order to address the dearth of government technical institute in the area and to enhance technical expertise in industry specific streams in order to benefit the tribal society.
Technical Overview: Government Polytechnic Marwahi operates 4 diploma courses namely Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science & Engineering and Artificial lntelligence with an intake of 60 students in each branch.

The institute aims to cater the following branch-specific goals:

Civil- To build competent diploma holders in Civil who can contribute in designing, planning and building infrastructure for modern society.
Mechanical Engineering- To produce skilled diploma holders who can contribute to development of efficient and sustainable mechanical system and devices to improve safety and quality of life.
Computer Science and Engineering- To impart programming skills and problem-solving skill in students to address the digital needs of society and industry.
Artificial Intelligence- To produce expertise in automatic complex processes and minimize human involvement in risky/critical applications and save time in this fast moving globe.
