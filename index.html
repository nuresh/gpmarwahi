ABOUT

Government Polytechnic Marwahi is established in the year 2024 in the district of Gaurela Pendra Marwahi. The institute is a 100% state government funded body imparting technical education to students of tribal area of Gaurela Pendra Marwahi. lt is situated in village Kumhari, district Gaurela Pendra Marwahi. The nearest railway station is Pendra Road which is 41 KM away from the institute and nearest airport is Bilasa Devi Kevat Airport at a distance of 145 KM from Government polytechnic Marwahi......
